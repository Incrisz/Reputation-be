## Starting an Audit

### Start Audit Page
- **Purpose**: Initiate a new business reputation audit
- **Key Features**:
  - **Business Information Form**:
    - **Website URL**: The business website URL (e.g., https://example.com)
    - **Business Name**: Official name of the business (e.g., Acme Digital Solutions)
    - **Industry**: Business category/industry type (e.g., Digital Marketing, E-commerce, Healthcare)
    - **Country**: Country where the business is located (e.g., United States)
    - **City**: City/municipality of the business location (e.g., San Francisco)
    - **Description** (Optional): Additional business description for validation and context
    - **Keywords**: Industry keywords and services (e.g., ["digital marketing", "SEO services", "social media management"])


**What You Can Do**:
- Input your business website URL and information
- Provide business details for accurate audit matching
- Add relevant keywords to focus the audit analysis

## Account Types & Permissions

### Free Plan User
- Limited to 5 audits per month
- Basic reporting
- Limited to 7-day history

### Pro Plan User
- 50 audits per month
- Up to 5 business profiles
- Advanced reporting
- 90-day history
- Priority support

### Enterprise Plan User
- Unlimited audits
- Unlimited business profiles
- White-label reports
- Unlimited history
- Dedicated account manager
- API access

## FAQ

**Q: How often should I run audits?**
A: We recommend monthly audits to track meaningful changes.

**Q: Can I export my audit results?**
A: Yes! All audit results can be exported as PDF reports.

**Q: Can I add multiple businesses?**
A: Yes, depending on your plan. Professional plan allows 5 businesses, Business and Enterprise plans allow unlimited.

**Q: What is included in the audit?**
A: Our audit covers online presence, review management, social media, SEO performance, and brand visibility.

**Q: How can I upgrade my plan?**
A: You can upgrade your plan anytime from the Profile Settings page.
